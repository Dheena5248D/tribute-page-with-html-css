technoblade

here is the time line of technoblade

  • 1999:Technoblade, whose real name is Dave, is born in California.
  • 2013:Technoblade starts creating content for Minecraft on YouTube, primarily showcasing his PvP skills.
  • 2015:Technoblade participates in his first Minecraft tournament, the UHC Reddit All-Star Game.
  • 2016:Technoblade wins the Minecraft Monday tournament, a weekly event hosted by YouTuber Keemstar.
  • 2017:Technoblade participates in the first Minecraft Championships tournament, representing team Cyan Creepers. The team finishes in third place.
  • 2018:Technoblade wins the Minecraft Monday tournament for a second time, along with his teammate Ph1LzA.
  • 2019:Technoblade sets a world record for the Minecraft speedrun category "Random Seed Glitchless Any%" with a time of 22 minutes and 18 seconds.
  • 2020:Technoblade wins the Minecraft Championship tournament, representing team Aqua Axolotls.
  • 2021:Technoblade announces that he has been diagnosed with a rare form of cancer, but he assures his fans that he will continue to create content and fight the disease. He participates in the MCC 14 tournament while undergoing treatment and helps his team, the Red Rabbits, win the event.
  • 2022:Technoblade lost his life to cancer
